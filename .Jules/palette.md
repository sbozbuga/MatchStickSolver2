## 2025-02-14 - Primary Action Affordance
**Learning:** Terminal-style inputs that rely solely on the Enter key for submission can be confusing for general users, especially on touch devices where the keyboard behavior varies. A visible "Solve" button provides a clear affordance.
**Action:** Always ensure primary actions have visible controls (buttons) in addition to keyboard shortcuts, and verify accessibility labels.

## 2026-02-23 - Visible Input Controls
**Learning:** Hidden shortcuts like "double-click to clear" are undiscoverable and inaccessible. Visible actions (like a clear button) within inputs significantly improve usability and accessibility for all users.
**Action:** Replace hidden shortcuts with visible, accessible controls.
